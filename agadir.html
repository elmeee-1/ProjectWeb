Discover Agadir

The Capital of the South, Sunshine, and Endless Beaches.

Landmark
The Old Fortress

Agadir Oufella

The historical ruins of the 16th-century Kasbah sitting high above the city. It offers the most spectacular panoramic views of the modern city layout, the sweeping bay, and the bustling commercial port.

Must Visit
Golden Sands

The Bay of Agadir

Renowned for its 300 days of sunshine a year. The massive, crescent-shaped beach is lined with a beautiful promenade perfect for jogging, relaxing at cafes, or simply enjoying the calm Atlantic waters.

Shopping
The Grand Market

Souk el Had

One of the largest markets in all of Africa. Protected by impressive high walls and multiple gates, it is a sensory overload of fresh produce, colorful spices, beautiful Moroccan rugs, and traditional clothing.

Luxury & Leisure
Modern Coastline

The Marina

A glamorous and modern district located at the end of the beach. Surrounded by luxury yachts, high-end boutiques, and fantastic international restaurants with direct views over the water.

Surfing Hub
Coastal Village

Taghazout Vibes

Located just north of the city, this world-famous fishing village has become a legendary destination for surfers. Known for its perfect waves, bohemian atmosphere, and incredible seafood.

Local Heritage
Liquid Gold

The Capital of Argan

The Souss region is the global epicenter for Argan trees. Visit local women's cooperatives to see the traditional method of extracting this precious oil used for both culinary masterpieces and high-end cosmetics.
