Discover · Decide · Try

Activities
in Morocco

From the misty Rif mountains of the North to the golden dunes of the South,
and the sparkling Atlantic shores — Morocco offers adventures for every traveller.

North South Beaches
01 — Northern Morocco

North Morocco
Activities

The north blends Andalusian heritage, Rif Mountain wilderness, and ancient medinas. A region of striking contrasts — blue cities, cedar forests, and Atlantic cliffs.

Walking Tour — Chefchaouen
Chefchaouen, Rif Mountains
Wander the iconic blue-painted alleyways of the medina, visit the Kasbah museum, and hike up to the Spanish mosque for a panoramic view of the blue city.
150 MAD / person
~3–4 hours
Easy
Cedar Forest Hike — Azrou
Azrou, Middle Atlas
Trek through ancient cedar forests where Barbary macaques roam freely. Guides lead you through trails with stunning Atlas mountain views and wildlife encounters.
200 MAD / person
~5–6 hours
Moderate
Fès Medina Guided Tour
Fès el-Bali, Fès
Explore the world's largest car-free urban area. Visit the Chouara Tannery, Al-Qarawiyyin Mosque (oldest university), and hidden fondouks with a certified local guide.
350 MAD / person
~4 hours
Easy
Snorkeling & Boat Tour
Al Hoceima National Park
Discover crystal-clear Mediterranean waters aboard a local fishing boat. Snorkel among colourful reefs and isolated coves accessible only by sea.
400 MAD / person
~3 hours
Easy
Surfing Lessons
Moulay Bousselham, Atlantic
Beginner-friendly surf lessons on the Atlantic coast just north of Rabat. Boards and wetsuits included. Small groups of max 6 students per instructor.
500 MAD / person
~2 hours
Moderate
Skiing — Michlifen Resort
Ifrane, Middle Atlas
Morocco's premier ski resort sits at 2,000 m elevation. Ski or snowboard on groomed pistes from December to March. Rentals and lessons available on site.
500 MAD / day
Full day pass
Moderate
02 — Southern Morocco

South Morocco
Activities

The south is where the Sahara begins. Vast ergs, ancient kasbahs, starlit camps, and camel caravans await in one of the world's most dramatic landscapes.

Camel Trek — Erg Chebbi
Merzouga, Sahara Desert
Ride across the legendary orange dunes of Erg Chebbi at sunset. The package includes a guided camel trek, Berber camp dinner, traditional music, and overnight stay under the stars.
500 MAD / person
Sunset + overnight
Easy
Aït Ben Haddou Kasbah Tour
Ouarzazate, Drâa Valley
Explore the UNESCO World Heritage ksar of Aït Ben Haddou with a local historian. Climb to the granary at the top for sweeping valley views, and visit film studios nearby.
300 MAD / person
~2–3 hours
Easy
4×4 Desert Safari
Zagora & Erg Chigaga
Blast across remote desert tracks in a 4×4 Land Rover with an expert driver-guide. Reach the remote Erg Chigaga dunes, far from the tourist trail, for an authentic experience.
1500 MAD / vehicle
Full day · 4 seats
Moderate
Stargazing Night — Sahara
Merzouga Desert Camp
One of the darkest skies on Earth. An astrophysicist guide leads a 2-hour session with high-powered telescopes. Spot the Milky Way, nebulae, and planets with the naked eye.
400 MAD / person
~2 hours · night
Easy
Toubkal Summit Trek
Toubkal, High Atlas
Summit North Africa's highest peak at 4,167 m. A 2-day guided ascent from Imlil village with mule support for luggage. Includes mountain hut accommodation at base camp.
1800 MAD / person
2 days · all-inclusive
Challenging
Argan Oil Farm Visit
Agadir – Sous-Massa
Tour a family-run argan cooperative. Watch Berber women hand-crack argan nuts using traditional stone mills and discover the full production process — finishing with tastings and shopping.
Free (with purchase)
~1 hour
Easy
03 — Moroccan Beaches

Beach Activities
& Coastal Escapes

Morocco is bordered by two seas — the Atlantic and the Mediterranean. From wild surf breaks to calm turquoise coves, its coastline is one of Africa's most diverse.

Surfing Capital — Taghazout
Taghazout, near Agadir
Morocco's surf mecca. World-class point breaks like Anchor Point and Killer Point attract pros and beginners alike. Week-long surf camps include lessons, board rental, and accommodation.
600 MAD / day
Lesson + board
Moderate
Kitesurfing — Dakhla
Dakhla Lagoon, Western Sahara
Dakhla's shallow lagoon and consistent trade winds make it one of the world's top kitesurfing spots. IKO-certified instructors run courses from beginner to advanced.
900 MAD / session
3-hour session
Moderate
Sailing Day Trip — Essaouira
Port of Essaouira
Sail along the Atlantic coast from the historic port of Mogador. Spot dolphins, sea turtles, and purple islands (Île Purpuraire). Includes lunch on board and snorkelling stop.
750 MAD / person
Full day · lunch incl.
Easy
Diving — Mediterranean Coast
Al Hoceima / M'Diq
Explore underwater caves, Posidonia meadows, and vivid marine life in the protected waters of Al Hoceima National Park. PADI-certified dive centres available locally.
550 MAD / dive
Equipment included
Moderate
Beach Volleyball + Hammam
Agadir Beach, Souss Bay
Join a beach volleyball tournament on Agadir's 10 km beach, then unwind in a traditional hammam — a steam bath followed by kessa scrub and argan oil massage.
250 MAD / person
Half day combo
Easy
Sunset Horseback Ride
Sidi Kaouki Beach, Essaouira
Gallop along one of Morocco's wildest Atlantic beaches at golden hour. Experienced stable provides well-trained horses for all levels, with a guide leading the 90-minute coastal ride.
480 MAD / person
~90 minutes
Easy
