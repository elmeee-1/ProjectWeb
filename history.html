Volubilis

Definition: A Roman outpost standing as a testament to North Africa's colonial antiquity.

Built in the 3rd century BC, Volubilis is the best-preserved Roman ruin in Morocco. It was a center for olive oil production and regional administration.

Koutoubia Mosque

Definition: The spiritual heartbeat of Marrakech, standing tall since the 12th century.
