Discover Tanger

The Gateway to Africa, Where Two Seas Meet.

Landmark
Ancient Fortress

The Kasbah

Perched high on the hill, the Kasbah offers incredible views over the Strait of Gibraltar. Wander through its ancient gates, explore the beautiful Dar el Makhzen museum, and get lost in the brilliant white and blue alleys.

Must Visit
Where Seas Meet

Cap Spartel

Stand at the exact point where the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea crash together. The iconic yellow lighthouse provides one of the most stunning, breezy panoramas in all of Northern Morocco.

Culinary
Legendary Tea

Café Hafa

Open since 1921, this legendary cliffside café is famous for its sweet mint tea and terraced seating. It has hosted famous writers, rock stars, and travelers looking for the perfect view across the water to Spain.

Natural Wonder
Mythical Geography

Caves of Hercules

Explore this fascinating natural sea cave complex located just outside the city. The most famous feature is its seaward opening, which was carved by the ocean waves into the distinct shape of the continent of Africa.

Botanical Retreat
Coastal Forest

Perdicaris Park

A lush, sprawling forest park overlooking the sea. Filled with exotic plants and winding hiking trails, it's the perfect escape from the bustling city center for a quiet afternoon walk in nature.

City Hub
The Meeting Place

Grand Socco

The vibrant heart of Tangier where the old medina meets the modern city. Surrounded by palm trees, bustling cafes, and the historic Cinema Rif, it’s the ultimate spot for people-watching and soaking up the local culture.

From the breezy terraces of Café Hafa to the historic walls of the Kasbah. Discover how our professional guides can help you experience the true magic of the North.
